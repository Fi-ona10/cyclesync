# Marketing Tests & Surveys

This folder contains marketing experiments, campaign documentation, and competitive research for Syncycle. It is the central reference for campaign results, A/B tests, and market analysis.

## Contents

- **Early Access Email Campaign.md** — Documentation of the branded email campaign sent to early access subscribers (April 30 – May 4, 2026), including design process, Mailchimp setup, and results (40% open rate, 13.3% click rate).
- **Syncycle Partner Access — Instagram A/B Ad Test** (`syncycle_partner_access_ab_test_summary.md`) — A/B test summary for the Partner Access feature launch on Instagram (May 7–8, 2026).
- **Competitor Landscape - Market Gaps.pdf** — Analysis of the competitive landscape and identified market gaps.
