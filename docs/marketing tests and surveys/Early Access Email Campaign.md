As part of the product launch, the goal of this task was to design and send a branded email campaign to users who had previously signed up for early access through the Syncycle website.

The work began with writing the email copy — defining the tone, structure, and messaging to match the Syncycle brand voice: supportive, science-backed, and empowering, like a knowledgeable friend rather than a clinical authority. The email welcomed early access subscribers, briefly introduced Syncycle and its three core features (Insights, Ask Fiona, and Partner Access), and directed users to create an account.

Once the copy was finalized, an HTML email template was designed from scratch. The design was iterated on several times to align with Syncycle's visual identity: a dark deep-purple color palette, Inter typography, glassmorphism-style UI elements, and feature cards with emoji icons chosen for maximum compatibility across email clients. The final template was built to be used in Mailchimp.

To support the Mailchimp build, the email header — featuring the branded gradient background and headline — was also exported as a retina-ready PNG for use as an image block.

The email was sent to 15 early access subscribers between April 30 and May 4, 2026, and achieved the following results:

* Delivery rate: 100% (15/15 delivered)
* Open rate: 40% (6 unique opens)
* Click rate: 13.3% (2 clicks)
* Clicks per unique open: 33.3%
* Bounce rate: 0%
* Unsubscribe rate: 0%

The 40% open rate and 13.3% click rate both significantly exceed typical email marketing benchmarks (industry average open rate ~21%, click rate ~2–3%), suggesting strong audience engagement from the early access cohort.
