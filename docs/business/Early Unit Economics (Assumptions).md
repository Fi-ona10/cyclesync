# Early Unit Economic (Assumptions)

> These numbers are early assumptions based on benchmarks from consumer apps like Flo, Calm, and Headspace, and will be validated once we start acquiring real users.

## Metrics

| Metric | Benchmark* | Syncycle assumption |
|---|---|---|
| CAC | €20–70 | €30 |
| CLV | €80–250 | €90** |
| CLV:CAC | 3–5x | 3x |

\* Benchmarked with Flo, Clue, Natural Cycles, Calm, Headspace

\*\* Assumption: €4.99/month subscription, 18 months avg. retention

## CAC Calculation

- Paid mobile ads typically cost €2–€4 per install
- Only ~1–2% of installs convert to paid users (consumer app benchmark)
- Our launch relies heavily on organic channels:
  - University communities
  - Social media content
  - Reddit
  - Product Hunt
- Paid ads support growth but are not the main acquisition channel *(for early launch)*
